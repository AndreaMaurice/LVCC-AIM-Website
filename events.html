EVENTS & ACTIVITES

BSIS & ACT Program Orientation

Before a semester begins, every course conducts a program orientation. The Program Orientation is exclusive to the College Department only and does not include Senior High and Junior High Students. This includes the announcement of upcoming events and rediscussion of the organization's mission and vision. Also an announcement about the beginning of the campaigns of new AIM Officers will also be brought up to encourage the newly enrolled students to run for a position as an AIM Officer.

Youth for IT (Y4IT)

Youth for IT is a 3-day event hosted by the UP System Information Technology Foundation for computer-related professions and technology. This is a nationwide event which is attended by IT professionals, teachers, students, and enthusiasts. The event is composed of different series of topics presentations from professionals around the globe. The first ever Online Y4IT was conducted over the span of three weeks, on three Saturdays.

ICT Week

This event is an Annual Celebration for ICT Major students of La Verdad Christian College of Apalit, Pampanga. Started in the year of December 2017. The celebration includes activities suchas seminars from invited IT Professionals, hackathons, programming competitions, and as well as sports that help build bond amongst team members.
A little fun fact, the first ever ICT Week was was put together with the help of the following: Lois Lane, Mike Agamin, Aiza Abcede, Jeff Pecana, Giselle Dionisio, Krystal Cruz, Yves Marin, Racquel Salumbides, Lemuel Florentino, Christine Larazo.
