EVENTS & ACTIVITES

BSIS & ACT Program Orientation

Before a semester begins, every course conducts a program orientation. The Program Orientation is exclusive to the College Department only and does not include Senior High and Junior High Students. This includes the announcement of upcoming events and rediscussion of the organization's mission and vision. Also an announcement about the beginning of the campaigns of new AIM Officers will also be brought up to encourage the newly enrolled students to run for a position as an AIM Officer.

Youth for IT (Y4IT)

Youth for IT or Y4IT is a 3-day event hosted by the UP System Information Technology Foundation for computer-related professions and technology. This is a nationwide event which is attended by IT professionals, teachers, students, and enthusiasts. The event is composed of different series of topics presentations from professionals around the globe. The first ever Online Y4IT was conducted on the dates November 21, December 5, December 12 on the Year 2020. The event was conducted over the span of three weeks on three Saturdays.

ICT Week

This event is an Annual Celebration for ICT Major students of La Verdad Christian College of Apalit, Pampanga. Started in the year of December 2017. The celebration includes activities such as seminars from invited IT Professionals, a plethora of competitions from pageants and singing competitions to programming competitions and even quiz bees.

A little fun fact, the first ever ICT Week was was put together with the help of the following: Lois Lane, Mike Agamin, Aiza Abcede, Jeff Pecana, Giselle Dionisio, Krystal Cruz, Yves Marin, Racquel Salumbides, Lemuel Florentino, Christine Larazo.
