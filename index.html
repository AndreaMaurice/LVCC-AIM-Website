Association of ICT Majors

This organization aims to provide holistic support to ICT majors for enhancing their skills and producing highly competent, disciplined and professional graduates. We envision to be a school organization that looks after the welfare of ICT Majors in line with the school's thrust of supporting academic excellence and upholding moral standards.
